How to Use Golden Grammar

Getting started with Golden Grammar is simple. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Paste Your Text

Copy the text you want to check and paste it into the text box. You can check up to 700 words at a time.

Step 2: Click "Check Grammar"

Click the button and our AI will analyze your writing in seconds, identifying grammar, spelling, and punctuation issues.

Step 3: Review the Results

Your corrected text will automatically appear in the text box. Below, you'll see what changes were made and why.

Step 4: Copy Your Corrected Text

Click "Copy Text" to copy your corrected text to your clipboard.
