How to Use Golden Grammar

Getting started with Golden Grammar is simple and straightforward. Follow these steps to improve your writing:

Step 1: Paste Your Text

Copy the text you want to check and paste it into the text box on our grammar checker page. You can check up to 700 words at a time, which is perfect for emails, blog posts, essays, and more.

Step 2: Click "Check Grammar"

Once your text is in the box, simply click the "Check Grammar" button. Our advanced AI will analyze your writing in seconds, identifying potential issues with grammar, spelling, punctuation, and style.

Step 3: Review the Results

Golden Grammar will highlight issues in your text and provide clear explanations for each one. You'll see what type of error it is (grammar, spelling, punctuation, or style) along with suggestions for improvement.

Step 4: Make Your Edits

Review each suggestion and decide which changes to make. You can copy the corrected version or manually edit your original text based on our recommendations.

Tips for Best Results

For the most accurate grammar checking, try to submit complete sentences and paragraphs rather than fragments. The more context our AI has, the better it can understand your intended meaning and provide helpful suggestions.

If you're working on a longer document, break it into sections of 700 words or less and check each section separately. This ensures thorough analysis of your entire piece.
