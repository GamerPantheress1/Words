What Is Word Count Hero?

Word Count Hero is a free online word counter designed for students, writers, bloggers, and professionals who need fast, accurate text analysis. Instantly count words, characters (with or without spaces), sentences, paragraphs, and estimated reading time — all in real time.

Your text never leaves your device. Everything is processed locally for complete privacy.

Word Count Guides & Common Questions

How Many Words Is a Page?

A standard page contains about 250–300 words single-spaced or 500 words double-spaced. Use Word Count Hero to calculate exact length.

How Many Words Is a 5 Page Essay?

A 5-page essay is typically 1,250–1,500 words depending on spacing and formatting.

Characters to Words Converter

On average, one word equals 4–5 characters including spaces. Our tool calculates both instantly.

Reading Time Calculator

Most readers consume text at ~200 words per minute. Word Count Hero estimates reading time automatically.
